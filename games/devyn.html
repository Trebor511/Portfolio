Devyn in Space

FPS Wave Shooter • Unity 2022 • C#

Overview

Devyn in Space is a first-person wave-based shooter developed in Unity 2022 as a six-person group project. The game features multiple combat arenas, a complete weapon and upgrade system, varied enemy behaviors, and boss encounters designed to challenge the player across progressive waves.

The project was scoped and completed as a fully playable experience, including four unique weapons, four standard enemy types, two boss fights, and an in-game shop system that allows players to upgrade stats and acquire new weapons between waves. The game emphasizes clear combat readability, escalating difficulty, and replay-focused systems.

Core Gameplay Systems

  • Wave System: Designed and implemented the enemy wave logic, including progression, escalation rules, and wave completion conditions.
  • Weapon System: A modular weapon framework supporting four distinct guns with different firing behaviors, ammo handling, and balance profiles.
  • Enemy AI: Multiple enemy archetypes with unique behaviors and combat roles to create varied encounters.
  • Upgrade & Shop System: A complete in-game shop allowing players to purchase weapons and upgrades between waves.
  • Save System: Persistent data handling to save player progression and unlocked content.

Team Structure & My Role

Devyn in Space was developed by a team of six. I served as the Lead Developer on the project, responsible for guiding technical decisions, organizing the overall structure of the codebase, and coordinating system implementation across the team.

In addition to leadership responsibilities, I worked as a generalist gameplay programmer, contributing directly to the design and implementation of multiple core systems. This included both hands-on programming and mentoring teammates to ensure features were completed, integrated, and stable.

Enemy Design & Behavior

A significant portion of my development time was focused on designing and implementing enemy behaviors that felt distinct and readable during high-action combat scenarios.

  • Melee Enemies: Close-range attackers designed to pressure the player’s positioning and movement.
  • Juggernauts: High-health enemies that absorb damage and force prioritization during intense waves.
  • Swarm Enemies: Small, fast attackers intended to overwhelm the player through numbers rather than raw strength.
  • Ranged Enemies: Distance-based attackers that introduce positional threats and encourage spatial awareness.

Level Design & Environmental Systems

I was responsible for the level design of all four combat levels as well as both boss arenas. Each space was designed to support wave-based gameplay, emphasizing clear sightlines, player movement options, and combat flow.

  • Four fully designed combat levels
  • Two dedicated boss arenas
  • Ladder traversal system
  • Damage and disappearing platform mechanics
  • Pickups for health and weapons
